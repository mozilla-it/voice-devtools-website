Projects

The Open Innovation team directly manages several product, technology and data focused initiatives and programs. We also help Mozilla connect with the expertise, creativity, capacity and influence of existing and new communities who align with Mozilla’s mission, and we work hard to ensure Mozilla's communities are safe, supportive, and all around fantastic.

Voice

Alongside Mozilla’s Emerging Technologies team, we are driving transformational product and ecosystem development around the Voice market. Our goal is for voice experiences to be private, open, and accessible to Everyone.

This work includes fostering the creation of a fully open set of foundational voice technologies, such as the open source Deep Speech speech-to-text Engine, our text-to-speech engine, and Common Voice, the world's largest database of open voice data.

Managed by the Open Innovation team, Common Voice is a globally crowdsourced project.

Mozilla Support

We manage Mozilla’s Support program, delivering customer support at the right time, in the right place, and in the right way. Support at Mozilla is driven by a global community of volunteers who answer questions across multiple channels and know as much about our products as any employee (perhaps even more!).

Community Engagement

We build, engage, and support our contributor communities through various programs and infrastructure. Thousands of people around the world beta test Mozilla's newest technologies and tackle timely problems by subscribing to our campaigns newsletter. Our Mission-driven Mozillian program aims to create a modern unified experience for anyone who wants to support Mozilla by evangelizing, teaching, advocating, localizing, documenting and community building.

IAM for Contributor systems

We believe that All Mozillians (paid staff and contributors) need to have convenient and appropriate access to Mozilla services through a unified, authoritative, integrated identity system that empowers them to build a better Internet. That is why we co-developed the Identity and Access Management program which aims to better integrate contributors into the Mozilla organization and workflows.
